Delete your SENSEL account and data

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Delete inside the app

  1. Open SENSEL.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Go to Your data.
  4. Tap Delete account permanently.
  5. Confirm deletion.

This deletes your authentication account and associated account-linked cloud data.

Request deletion without the app

Send an email from the address used for your SENSEL account to sensel.scentapp@gmail.com with the subject SENSEL account deletion request.

Include only:

  • the account email address;
  • a clear request to delete the account and associated data.

We may ask you to verify control of the account email before processing. We aim to complete verified requests within 30 days, unless a shorter period is required by law.

Data deleted

  • authentication account and profile;
  • Scent Captures and saved scent content;
  • saved directions and decisions;
  • preference snapshots and preference-learning state;
  • feedback events and account-linked analytics;
  • account-linked Premium-interest records.

Data that may remain

We may retain limited records where required by law or reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or proof that a deletion request was completed. Data that is fully anonymized and cannot reasonably be linked back to you may also remain.

Guest data

Guest data is stored locally on the device. Clear it in Settings or uninstall SENSEL. Because guest data is not tied to an account, we cannot remove it remotely.
