Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SENSEL (“SENSEL,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), operated by Đinh Quốc Hùng in Vietnam, handles information when you use the SENSEL mobile application and related services.

1. What SENSEL is

SENSEL helps users record scent experiences, explore scent preferences, save scent directions, and make personal scent decisions. SENSEL is not a perfume store, medical service, or professional safety service.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: email address, authentication credentials handled by our authentication provider, and account identifiers.
  • User content: scent descriptions, Scent Captures, saved directions, decisions, preference snapshots, optional notes, and feedback you choose to save.
  • Premium-interest information: an email address only when you voluntarily submit it to register interest in Premium.

Information collected automatically

  • App activity: limited events such as onboarding completion, saving a direction, opening a reference section, creating a decision, or expressing Premium interest.
  • Device identifier: a randomly generated app-specific identifier used for basic product analytics. It is not an advertising ID.
  • Technical information: session, network, error, and security information needed to operate, protect, and troubleshoot the service.

3. How we use information

  • Provide authentication, cloud sync, saved content, and account recovery.
  • Interpret scent descriptions and generate scent-direction suggestions.
  • Maintain preference-learning features and improve recommendation quality.
  • Understand basic app usage, diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and deletion requests.

4. AI processing and consent

SENSEL uses Cloudflare as a third-party AI service provider. No scent-description text is transmitted to the AI service merely because you type it. Transmission begins only after you tap an AI action such as “Understand this scent” or “Understand this direction” and, on first use, affirmatively accept the in-app AI processing disclosure.

  • Scent Capture: the scent description you entered is transmitted to generate a scent interpretation.
  • Decision: the scent description, selected purpose, and vibe or avoidance context are transmitted to generate a direction interpretation.
  • Purpose: the transmitted text is used to provide the requested AI-assisted interpretation and related app functionality.
  • Safeguards: requests are sent over encrypted HTTPS connections. SENSEL attempts to redact obvious email addresses, phone numbers, social handles, and URLs before transmission.
  • Choice: you may decline the disclosure and continue using non-AI parts of SENSEL. You can reset the saved AI-processing choice in Settings.

SENSEL does not sell AI input, use it for third-party advertising, or use it to build advertising profiles. SENSEL does not intentionally create a separate permanent archive of raw AI requests; however, text you choose to save as a Scent Capture or Decision remains part of your saved app content until you delete it or your account. Cloudflare processes requests as a service provider under its applicable service terms and technical configuration. Avoid entering sensitive personal information into free-text scent fields.

5. Service providers and sharing

We use service providers to operate SENSEL:

  • Supabase: authentication, database, account management, and cloud synchronization.
  • Cloudflare: third-party AI processing and delivery of user-initiated interpretation requests.
  • Expo and platform services: application runtime, build, update, and notification functionality where enabled.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use third-party advertising or affiliate ranking. We may disclose information when required by law, to protect users or the service, or in connection with a lawful transfer of the service, subject to appropriate safeguards.

6. Data retention

Account-linked content is retained while your account remains active or as needed to provide the service. Local guest data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the app. Limited security, transaction, or deletion records may be retained when legally required or reasonably necessary to prevent fraud, resolve disputes, or demonstrate compliance.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Review or remove saved content within the app.
  • Clear local or synchronized app data in Settings.
  • Delete your account in Settings, which permanently deletes the authentication account and associated account-linked cloud data.
  • Use the public account deletion page when you cannot access the app.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or a copy of personal information, subject to applicable law.

8. Security

We use access controls, encrypted network connections, authenticated sessions, and database row-level controls where applicable. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International processing

SENSEL may process information in countries where our service providers operate. Those countries may have different data-protection laws. We use service-provider agreements and technical safeguards appropriate to the service.

10. Age

SENSEL is intended for users aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. Contact us if you believe a child under 16 has created an account.

11. Product references

Product names and official brand links are independent editorial references used to explain scent directions. They are not sponsored, ranked, affiliate, shopping, or purchase recommendations. External brand websites have their own privacy practices.

12. Changes

We may update this policy as SENSEL changes. We will update the date above and provide additional notice when legally required.
